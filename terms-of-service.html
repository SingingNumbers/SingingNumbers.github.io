2. Description of service

SingingNumbers is a free, fully offline calculator application that plays musical tones corresponding to each digit and operator entered. The App ships six user-facing screens reachable from the navigation drawer — Calculator (with a Big Calculator mode that extends the keypad to 24 notes), Saved, Scales, Octaves, Instruments, and Settings — and the App:

  • Performs arithmetic locally on your device.
  • Synthesises audio in real time on your device using Google Oboe and Android's audio output APIs (no microphone access).
  • Requires no internet connection.
  • Collects, stores, and transmits no personal data (see the Privacy Policy for the on-device-only preference and saved-calculations files).
  • Contains no advertisements.
  • Offers no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
